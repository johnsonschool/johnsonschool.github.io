About us

Welcome to Johnson English Medium Higher Secondary School

Johnson English Medium Higher Secondary School is Affiliated to the Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education, Bhopal. DISE Code: 23390224115

Johnson English Medium Higher Secondary School, Jabalpur is a Senior Secondary School (XI & XII) Affiliated to Madhya Pradesh Board, Bhopal. It was established in the year 1964. The school is a Coed Day School, with classes from Nursery to XII. It is an English Medium School. It was founded by the Deaconesses of Madhya Pradesh Regional Conference of the Methodist Church in India.

We believe that every child is a reservoir of talents and if given the right environment and impetus, he will blossom intellectually and holistically beyond our imagination.
