Fees Rules

  • Fees and other dues are to be cleared on or before 20th of each month. Fees are to be paid monthly. If fees are not paid for more than two months the name of the student is liable to be removed from the school register.
  • No fine will be charged if the fees of the month is paid upto the 20th of that month.If 20th is a holiday then fees without fine will be collected upto 21st of the month. From 21st till the last working day of the month a fine of Rs. 20/- will have to be paid. If still, the fees remains unpaid then a fine of Rs.50/- will have to be paid for each month or part thereof.
  • If the fees is not paid by the last working day of the month, the students will not be allowed to enter the class from the first working day of the next month.
  • In case a student is to be withdrawn from the school, one months notice will be required to be given by the parents or guardian, failing which one months fees in addition will be charged.
  • The transfer certificate will only be issued to a student after all dues are cleared.
  • Payment of fees and other dues must be made in cash only.
  • No refund of fees is allowed in any case and no deduction is made for any absence of period of a student from school.
  • Please render exact amount.
  • Students appearing for Home/Board examination will receive their admit cards after all dues have been cleared.
  • To obtain any certificate parents/guardians will have to apply three working days in advance.
